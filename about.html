About Us

A father, a son, and a workshop full of good ideas.

We're Colin and Carson Burns — a dad and his teenage son who happen to be really good at building websites and automations. We live in Aledo, Texas, and we help local businesses look great online and work smarter behind the scenes.

Why we started this

Here's the thing about us: we're not just builders — we're local business owners ourselves. Between the two of us we run Bearcat Turf, Middle School Matchup DFW West, and LNDMRK Drone. We know exactly what it feels like to need a website yesterday, juggle invoices, chase leads, and wish someone would just handle the tech so we could get back to running the business.

Colin spent the first twenty years of his career in marketing, working with brands like Red Bull, Chipotle, Match.com, and AT&T. He knows what makes a brand click with customers — and how much of that playbook local businesses never get access to because agencies price them out.

Meanwhile Carson — still in high school — was teaching himself how to build software, automate tasks, and ship real things with modern AI tooling. He was already better at it than most professionals twice his age.

One afternoon it just clicked: Colin understands brands, businesses, and buyers. Carson understands how to actually build the stuff. Put them together and you get a two-man team that can quote, design, build, and ship in a week — at a price that actually makes sense for a plumber, a salon owner, or a family-run restaurant.

That's BurnsBuilt. If it says Burns Built, we built it with our own hands (and a lot of smart tooling behind the scenes).
