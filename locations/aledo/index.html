Frequently asked

How much does a custom website cost?

Most marketing sites we build for local Aledo and DFW businesses land between $3,500 and $9,500 depending on page count, custom features, and integrations. Custom CRMs and internal tools start around $6,000 and scale with scope. We give a fixed quote up front — no hourly billing surprises.

How long does a website take?

Marketing sites: 5–7 business days from kickoff to live. Custom CRMs and internal tools: 2–3 weeks typically. We move fast because we don't run a four-person account team between you and the code.

Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy — why pay more for custom?

Templates are fine if your goal is "have a website." If your goal is "rank on Google for what my customers actually search," templates fall short — they're slow, they share infrastructure with thousands of other sites, and you can't extend them when your business needs something specific. A custom site is faster, ranks better, and grows with you.

Do you do SEO and ongoing maintenance?

Yes. Every site we ship is built with SEO baked in — clean HTML, schema markup, fast load times, sitemap, robots.txt, structured data. We also offer a monthly retainer for content updates, security patches, and ongoing optimization if you want it. Many clients self-manage after launch — we make that easy too.
