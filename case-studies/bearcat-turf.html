The brief

A 6-figure-install business relying entirely on referrals.

Bearcat Turf & Outdoors is a family-owned, woman-owned, HUB-certified artificial turf installer in Aledo, Texas, serving the Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex. When they came to us, their site was on a templated platform: slow, hard to update, generic copy, no measurable conversion path.

They were doing 6-figure installs by relying entirely on referrals — leaving organic search, paid search, and a real digital sales funnel on the table. They needed a sales tool, not a brochure.

What we built

A 186-page custom Astro site with deep integrations across the lead pipeline.

Not a template. A purpose-built sales engine that qualifies leads at intake, prices their project on the spot, surfaces relevant case studies, follows up automatically when they go cold, and writes the first draft of the owner's reply email.

Every inbound lead fans out in parallel to five destinations: customer estimate email, internal Slack/email notification, Quo CRM contact, MailerLite enrollment, and an AI-drafted reply ready for one-click send.

Key features

Ten features that compound into a sales pipeline.

Custom cost calculator

11.3% completion-to-lead conversion.

Multi-step, mobile-first, ZIP-gated, with real benchmark pricing by project type and tier. Industry typical: 1–3%. This one converts 4–10× over.

Builds rapport while qualifying budget and scope before the owner spends a minute on the lead.
AI lead qualifier

Personalized draft reply in 60 seconds.

Every inbound lead is read by Claude / Gemini. A personalized draft email lands in the owners' inbox ready to send — pulling per-lead context: project type, square footage, urgency, comparison signals.

Time-to-first-touch goes from hours to under a minute, with no template feel.
Batting cage configurator

Real-time pricing for backyard + commercial cages.

Live pricing engine for backyard, school, and training-facility cage builds. Cimarron dealer integration. Owners can see margin, the customer sees a defensible number.

Opens a second product line with its own qualified intake.
Baseball field ROI calculator

Email-gated PDF analysis for ADs.

Multi-field portfolio model with email-gated PDF analysis for school athletic directors and club programs. Multi-year cost-of-ownership comparison vs. natural grass.

Pulls high-ticket institutional leads that referrals can't reach.
24-hour scheduled follow-up

Personalized HTML email with 3 matched case studies.

Every cost-calculator submission receives a personalized HTML email 24 hours later with 3 case studies matched to their project type. Built on Resend's scheduled_at — no queue infrastructure needed.

Recovers the leads who almost-converted on first visit.
Semantic site search

Cmd+K natural-language search across 127 pages.

HuggingFace BGE embeddings, cosine-similarity ranking on every query. Users find "drainage on clay soil" without knowing the exact page title.

Lowers bounce on a site too deep to navigate by menu alone.
Bilingual EN/ES

Full Spanish landing flow, not auto-translated chrome.

Helsinki-NLP translation pipeline, full hreflang wiring, dedicated Spanish landing + contact flows. Spanish-speaking customers get a real experience, not a Google-translated half-effort.

Captures a meaningful slice of DFW that competitors don't speak to.
40+ city service-area pages

AI-batch-generated with differentiated content.

Each city page is unique — not a templated copy-paste. Neighborhood detail, local schema, its own conversion path. AI-batched but human-reviewed.

Ranking for "pet turf [city]" across the entire DFW metroplex.
Full conversion pipeline

10 lead-capture surfaces × 4 channels.

Meta Pixel + server-side Conversions API (via Cloudflare Worker), GA4 + GTM, Google Ads conversion tracking — wired into every form. Server-side recovers 30–40% of signal that iOS and Safari strip from browser pixels.

Lead routing infrastructure

One submission, five parallel destinations.

Every form submission fans out: customer estimate email, internal notification, Quo CRM contact, MailerLite enrollment, AI follow-up draft. All logged for durable audit.

Lead never falls through the cracks; ops never bottleneck on triage.
Technical highlights

A site the size of a SaaS, the speed of a landing page.

  • Cloudflare Workers as the application backbone. Every form, every API integration, every redirect lives in a single TypeScript Worker. No backend server, no database hosting bill, sub-50ms cold starts globally.
  • Astro SSG + Workers Sites. Entire site statically generated at build time and served from Cloudflare's edge. 186 pages, Tailwind utility classes, full Astro Image pipeline producing responsive WebP variants for every photo (~1,800 image variants at build).
  • AI integrations done right — not chatbots. Used for the work that actually moves the needle: alt-text generation (Gemini Vision), brand-voice lead reply drafts (Anthropic Claude), semantic search embeddings (HuggingFace BGE), translation pipelines (Helsinki-NLP), per-city page content (AI-batched).
  • Brand redesign mid-engagement. When the client commissioned a new brand identity — typographic wordmark, 7-color palette, 4-font stack, 60+ logo variants — we shipped the full visual migration across 200+ pages, 14 email templates, schema metadata, OG share cards, and favicons in a single coordinated deploy. CSS-variable architecture meant changing one file flipped the entire site's color scheme.
  • SEO infrastructure as code. JSON-LD generated per page from data sources, sitemap with lastmod timestamps, IndexNow ping pipeline (Bing/Yandex), 18 legacy-URL 301 redirects in the Worker, www→apex canonicalization, llms.txt for LLM-friendly content indexing.
