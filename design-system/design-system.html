07 · Voice & Tone

Plain-spoken.
Slightly stubborn.

We sound like a tradesperson who reads. Direct, specific, never showy. We don't oversell what software does and we don't undersell what people do. Short sentences. Real numbers. No empty adjectives.

2 voices · one shop

Sound like this

Do
  • Five days from kickoff to live site. Flat rate, no retainers.
  • We build the website. We hook up the bookings. You run the business.
  • Built for Aledo Roasters in 4 days. Order volume up 38% in the first month.
  • No contracts longer than the work.
  • If your site goes down at 11 PM, you call Matt. He picks up.

Not like this

Don't
  • Leveraging best-in-class AI to drive synergistic outcomes for SMBs.
  • Your trusted digital transformation partner on the journey.
  • We unlock value through innovative solutions tailored to you.
  • Reach out today and let's discuss your goals!
  • Empowering local businesses with cutting-edge technology.
Naming

BurnsBuilt

One word, two caps. Never "Burns Built" or "Burns-Built." The wordmark always pairs the cap-B's with the Twin Block when space allows.

Numbers

Specific, not rounded.

Say "4 days," not "fast." Say "+38% orders," not "growth." Real numbers earn trust; round numbers feel made up.

Punctuation

The middle dot · is ours.

Used in stamps, captions, and labels (Father · Son, TX · Est. 2026). Never use it in body copy — there it reads as fussy.
