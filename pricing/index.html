Frequently asked

Are there long-term contracts?

No. Monthly maintenance is month-to-month — cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We don't believe in locking customers in. If we're not delivering value, you should be free to leave.

What's included in monthly maintenance?

Hosting, SSL, security patches, uptime monitoring, and small content edits (phone number changes, new service additions, photo swaps, etc.). For larger changes — adding new pages or features — we quote separately so you're never surprised by a bigger invoice.

How long does a website take to build?

Basic and Standard tiers usually go live in 5–7 business days from kickoff. Custom builds depend on scope but most take 2–4 weeks. Automation projects: Starter and Growth tiers ship in 1–2 weeks; Custom CRM builds typically 2–4 weeks.

Do I own the website?

Yes, completely. The code, the domain, the content — all yours. If you ever decide to leave, we hand over everything. No "platform lock-in," no proprietary CMS you can't take with you.

What if I need something not listed in the tiers?

Tell us what you need and we'll quote it. Most projects fit cleanly into a tier, but we've also built custom AI tools, hyper-local SEO sites with 100+ pages, and multi-system CRM integrations. Quoted by scope, fixed price.

Do you charge for the initial consult?

No. The first call (20–30 minutes) is free. We use it to understand your business and figure out if we're a fit. If we are, you get a fixed quote within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer, ACH, or credit card (CC adds a 3% processing fee). Half upfront, half on launch for one-time setup. Monthly fees billed on the 1st via Stripe.

Can I upgrade or downgrade tiers later?

Yes. Move from Basic to Standard or Standard to Custom anytime — we just bill the difference. Same for automation tiers.

Ready to get a real quote?

Tell us about your business and what you need. Fixed quote back to you within one business day.
