Frequently asked

Most of my customers find me through Google reviews. Will a website help?

Yes — because Google's local pack ranks you on (1) review count + score, (2) site quality + speed, and (3) topical relevance. Reviews matter, but so does a fast site with proper service-area pages. They compound.

Can you set up emergency dispatch routing?

Yes. Forms tagged "emergency" or after-hours text-backs containing "URGENT" route directly to your on-call tech's phone. Non-emergency leads queue to morning. Configurable routing rules.

How fast does the site load on a phone?

Under 1 second on 4G. We use Cloudflare's edge network and aggressively optimize every image. Speed matters in HVAC — customers in a 105°F house don't wait.

Do you integrate with ServiceTitan / HouseCall Pro / Jobber?

Yes — webhooks from form submissions push directly into your dispatch software. We've worked with most of the major HVAC platforms.
