Frequently asked

I already have a website. Why would I rebuild?

Most landscaping websites we audit lose every search to national chains because they have one "Services" page. We build a page for every service-by-zip combination — Google rewards specificity. If your existing site ranks well, great; if not, that's the problem and that's what we fix.

How long until I see leads?

Direct traffic and ad-driven traffic: immediately on launch. Organic SEO from the local pages: 2–6 weeks for first rankings, 3–6 months to mature. The ad-tracking improvements (Meta CAPI) start working the day you ship.

Do you do landscaping before-and-after photo galleries?

Yes — and we make them load fast (most landscaping sites are killed by 10MB unoptimized photos). We compress and serve them properly so your site doesn't crawl on mobile.

Can you integrate with my existing CRM or quoting tool?

Almost always yes. We work with most CRMs (Jobber, Service Titan, HouseCall Pro, etc.) and can wire form submissions directly into your existing flow. If you've outgrown your CRM, we also build custom ones — see Bearcat HQ.
