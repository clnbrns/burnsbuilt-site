What "business automation" actually means

Forget the buzzwords. Business automation is just: making your business do the boring work for you. The texts that go out after a missed call. The follow-up email a day after a lead fills out your form. The Google review request when a job wraps up. The invoice reminder seven days after the due date. All the stuff you should be doing — but aren't, because you're running a business.

We hook your existing tools together, plug the gaps with custom code where it matters, and hand you a system that runs in the background. You get your time back. Your customers feel taken care of. Nothing falls through the cracks.

What we automate

Lead follow-up

Automatic text within 60 seconds of a lead filling out your form. Email follow-up sequence over the next week if they don't respond. You stay top-of-mind without lifting a finger.

Appointment reminders

Text and email reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before any scheduled job or meeting. Reduces no-shows by 40–60% in most service businesses.

Missed-call text-back

If a customer calls and you can't pick up, an automatic text fires within 30 seconds — "Hey, sorry we missed you, what can we help with?" Most businesses recover 30%+ of missed calls this way.

Invoice & payment reminders

Automatic reminders 3, 7, 14, and 30 days after an invoice goes unpaid. Configurable tone (friendly → firm). Cash collection without the awkwardness.

Review collection

Once a job is marked complete, a Google review request fires the next morning with a one-tap link. More 5-star reviews, automatically.

Custom CRMs & internal tools

If your business has outgrown spreadsheets and Gmail, we build a custom dashboard. Lead inbox, pipeline, automated scoring. (See Bearcat HQ — we replaced spreadsheets + Gmail + QuickBooks for one client in 3 weeks.)
